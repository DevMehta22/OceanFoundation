The Ocean Foundation is a non-profit organization that was established in 2002 to support, strengthen, and promote organizations that are dedicated to conserving and protecting the world's oceans. The foundation's main objective is to create a healthy ocean and a sustainable ocean environment for present and future generations.

The Ocean Foundation is a vital organization that is playing a significant role in protecting the world's oceans. Its dedication to marine conservation and sustainability has helped to promote public awareness of the threats facing our oceans and has provided support to organizations that are working to address these threats. The foundation's commitment to creating a healthy and sustainable ocean environment for future generations is commendable, and we can all do our part to support its mission.
